Sus datos personales aportados en https://openswad.org/ serán tratados por OpenSWAD.org con la finalidad de gestionar actividades académicas on-line y su seguimiento, así como el desarrollo de tutorías y evaluaciones.
Su fotografía, aportada a su ficha personal, puede adquirir la condición de "pública" en caso de que así lo indique, de forma que pueda ser vista por otros estudiantes y profesores identificados en la plataforma, lo que consiente mediante la marcación de la casilla situada junto a su imagen. Your personal data provided on https://openswad.org/ will be processed by OpenSWAD.org with the purpose of managing on-line academic activities and their monitoring, as well as the development of mentoring and assessments.
Your photograph, attached to your personal profile, can acquire the status of "public" if you so indicate, so it can be seen by other students and teachers logged in the platform, which you agree with the marking of the box next to your photograph. If you do not check that box, your photograph is only visible to the teachers of the courses in which you are enrolled as a student, and to the students and teachers of those courses in which you are teacher.
You can exercise your right of access, rectification, cancellation and opposition to OpenSWAD.org throught email openswad@gmail.com.
